Welcome To Rock Paper Scissors Game

How to Play

Select the number of rounds you would like to play to start the game.

Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors to compete against the computer.

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

The player with the most wins after the selected number of rounds is the winner!



Note: The game will begin automatically once you select the number of rounds to play.
